Approach

How I work

🔍
Discovery First
▼
I go into stores, run workshops, and talk to users before writing a single user story. Real problems lead to real solutions — not assumptions.
📐
Clarity over Complexity
▼
Complex requirements deserve simple, well-scoped user stories. I translate business ambiguity into acceptance criteria that engineering can build against confidently.
📊
Decisions by Data
▼
Every prioritization decision is backed by operational KPIs, root cause analysis, or customer evidence — not gut feel or the loudest voice in the room.
🤝
Stakeholder as Partner
▼
I treat stakeholders as co-owners of the roadmap. Alignment built early means fewer surprises at release — and faster trust over time.
🔁
Ship, Learn, Iterate
▼
170+ enhancements weren't delivered in one go. Consistent, incremental delivery builds platform confidence and keeps momentum with business teams.
🤖
AI-Augmented Ownership
▼
I use Microsoft Copilot and generative AI tools to accelerate documentation, user story generation, and sprint reporting — so more time goes to thinking, less to writing.
